About Confidential Trading

Building bridges between brands and communities across Nepal.

Our Story

Confidential Trading, established in 2021, is a distinguished importing and distribution company dedicated to serving the Nepalese market with fresh, high-quality products. Our commitment revolves around providing exceptional value by carefully selecting superior goods that meet the diverse needs of consumers. With a clear vision and strong dedication, we aim to become a trusted bridge between global brands and the Nepalese marketplace, ensuring consistency in quality and satisfaction.

Our team boasts an impressive blend of expertise, with over 20 years of experience in finance and banking policy, enabling us to manage our operations with strong financial discipline and strategic foresight. Complemented by more than 15 years of experience in importing and logistics, our professionals ensure seamless and efficient supply chain management. This robust foundation allows us to handle even the most complex distribution challenges with precision and reliability.

In addition, our warehousing and product storage capabilities are supported by 15 years of specialized training, allowing us to maintain optimal product quality and inventory management. Our sales and marketing division, also enriched with over 15 years of experience, expertly navigates the nuances of Nepalese markets including general trade, modern retail, and HORECA sectors. This comprehensive knowledge ensures effective market penetration and brand representation.

Driven by a motivated and consumer-focused team, Confidential Trading excels in selecting and distributing FMCG, HORECA, and non-food products from around the world. Our deep understanding of product trends and market demands, combined with a passion for excellence, positions us uniquely to cater to the evolving needs of Nepalese consumers. We pride ourselves on nurturing strong relationships with partners and customers alike, fostering growth and mutual success in the dynamic marketplace.
